Our Story

The Heart of Glassblowing in Greater Houston

Glassblowing Houston is the only open-access glassblowing studio in the greater Houston area. We exist as a creative center to share the knowledge and enthusiasm of glass and serve the public.

We are a family-operated small business. Each of us is developing our voice, leading us down different avenues of creativity. We believe that the future requires continuously innovating and evolving to supply the public with enriching learning and artistic opportunities.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I just walk in and make glass?
We use an online booking system to ensure we have sufficient glass makers to serve you. You are welcome to walk-in on Saturdays and Sundays, but we are typically booked to capacity.
Do I need prior experience?
Not at all! Our experiences are designed for complete beginners through experienced makers. No matter where you are in your creative journey, you'll find our experiences accessible and enjoyable.
How can I contact you?
You can stop by the studio in person, chat with us via our website, call us at 832-559-3339, or email us at [email protected]
What are your Terms and Conditions?
Everyone working in our studios must read & sign our online safety waiver. We have a no refund policy but you can reschedule with no charge with 72 hours notice. Arrive 15 minutes early for your reservation.
Can I take my glass home the same day?
Glass made in the studio must cool slowly so you cannot take it home that day. The glass makers will estimate when it will be ready for pickup — it will be at least 24 hours from the time made.
What payment methods do you accept?
We love cash or check, but we accept all forms of credit card.
